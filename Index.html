About PiggyBank

PiggyBank is your personal finance companion on the journey to financial well-being. At PiggyBank, we believe that managing your money should be simple, intuitive, and even a bit fun. Our platform is designed to empower you to take control of your finances, set and achieve savings goals, and make informed decisions about your income and expenses.

Key Features

Seamlessly add, edit, and track your various income sources, ensuring a clear understanding of your financial inflow.



Categorize and manage your expenses effortlessly. PiggyBank helps you stay on top of where your money is going, allowing you to make smarter spending choices.



Set realistic savings goals and watch your progress unfold. Whether you're saving for a vacation, an emergency fund, or a special purchase, PiggyBank is here to support your financial aspirations.



Gain valuable insights into your spending patterns and financial habits through our detailed reports and visualizations. Understand trends, identify opportunities for improvement, and celebrate your financial victories.



Tailor PiggyBank to your preferences with customizable settings. Rest assured, your financial data is treated with the utmost security and privacy.
